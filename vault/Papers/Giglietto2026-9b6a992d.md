# Synthetic seduction: Evolving visual persuasion in coordinated online gambling promotion with generative {AI}

## Summary

This paper examines how coordinated networks promoting online gambling on Facebook deploy visual persuasion, and how generative AI has reshaped that promotional ecosystem since ChatGPT's public release. Drawing on more than 2,300 images from 223 gambling-focused groups, the authors combine large language models, embeddings, and clustering to build an empirical taxonomy of recurring persuasive drivers. They document an exponential surge in posting volume after late 2022 and argue that generative AI has intensified — rather than fundamentally reinvented — established manipulation tactics, exposing significant gaps in platform governance and regulatory frameworks.

## Key Contributions

- An empirical taxonomy of seven visual persuasion drivers used in coordinated gambling promotion.
- Quantitative evidence of a dramatic increase in coordinated promotional activity coinciding with the diffusion of generative AI tools.
- A scalable interpretive methodology combining LLMs, embedding-based representations, and clustering for visual content analysis.
- A case-grounded contribution to debates on synthetic media, manipulation, and the inadequacy of current platform and regulatory governance.

## Methods

The authors assembled a corpus of 2,300+ images from 223 coordinated Facebook gambling-focused groups. Images were characterized using large language models and embedding representations, then grouped via clustering to surface recurring visual and thematic patterns. They complement this with temporal statistical analysis of posting volume around ChatGPT's November 2022 release and qualitative interpretation of representative examples.

## Findings

- Seven recurring persuasive drivers emerged: aspirational wealth imagery, manufactured trust signals, urgency conditioning, cultural localisation, gamification, celebrity endorsements, and exploitation of social relations.
- Monthly posting volume in these networks grew from roughly 2,121 to 280,952 posts after ChatGPT's introduction.
- Generative AI appears to amplify and intensify existing persuasion tactics rather than introduce qualitatively new ones.
- Synthetic media enables more immersive imagery and more elaborate narrative framing in promotional content.
- Existing platform moderation and regulatory regimes are poorly equipped to address AI-amplified coordinated promotion.

## Connections
